NetErnels


Here We are showing a tutorial on how to install a NetErnels Kernel On your phone with TWRP.


In this tutorial we use the kernel designed for device with codename "gucci".


1.First You need to download the gucci kernel which is available on our Download page.

Then you need to boot into your your Recovery Mode. Any Recovery such as TWRP or OrangeFox will do the work.

Here we use TWRP in this tutorial.

Now as you boot into TWRP, you will see an interface as given below.





Now you need to select our kernel.We recommend you to put it in your /sdcard directory so it will be easy for you to find




Now click on the zip file you have downloaded earlier, and you will see a screen like this.Please do not enable the "Zip Signature Verification" as it may say an invalid file and thus may not install the kernel.Now swipe the button at bottom to flash.




As soon as you swipe, you will see our logo and the installer installing our kernel.Wait for the process to complete.




And when it completed you will be presented with a screen like this. And then reboot the system.
